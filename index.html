Welcome to CODESPARKS

Programming is, quite literally, all around us. From the take-out we order, to the movies we stream, code is ever present in our lives. Whether you are about to start your journey as a web developer, can’t decide which language to learn first, or just want to increase your digital literacy, learning programming fundamentals will be beneficial to your career. In this series of lessons, you’ll explore ideas that are at the heart of learning how to code and start writing your own code!.
